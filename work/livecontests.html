What separates Brandbook from other top UX and web design agencies?

We differentiate ourselves through our user-centered approach and in-depth expertise in UX and web design. Our team is skilled in all aspects of digital product creation, from user research to development.

Can your UX design agency help me with web design?

Yes, we specialize in both UX and web design to ensure a cohesive and user-friendly digital experience across all platforms.

What are your areas of focus as a UI/UX and web design agency?

Our focus includes mobile app design, web applications, enterprise software, and emerging tech like AR/VR and wearables, covering all stages from product strategy to development.

Do you do branding or should I hire a separate branding agency?

Yes, we offer branding services. Our team can help you create a cohesive and impactful brand identity that aligns with your business goals. However, if you're looking for specialized, in-depth branding work, you may also consider working with a dedicated branding agency. We can collaborate with them or handle branding directly, depending on your needs.

How much does it cost to hire you for a UI/UX design project?

The cost of a UI/UX design project varies depending on the scope, complexity, and specific requirements of your project. We offer customized solutions tailored to each client's needs, so prices can range widely. To get an accurate estimate, feel free to reach out to us with the details of your project, and we’d be happy to provide a proposal.

Do you work with startups? If so, how can my early‑stage company afford you?

Yes, we work with startups and love helping early-stage companies bring their ideas to life. We understand the unique challenges that startups face, especially around budget constraints. To accommodate startups, we offer flexible engagement models and scalable solutions to fit various budgets. We’re committed to finding the right balance between quality and cost, so you can get a great product without overspending. Reach out, and we’d be happy to discuss options that work for you.

Can you help us redesign our legacy enterprise/B2B software?

Absolutely! We specialize in transforming legacy enterprise and B2B software into modern, user-friendly, and efficient digital products. Our team has extensive experience working with complex systems, ensuring the redesigned product not only meets current usability standards but also aligns with your business goals. We approach these projects with a deep understanding of enterprise workflows and user requirements, creating solutions that drive productivity, adoption, and satisfaction among your users. Let's work together to breathe new life into your legacy software.

What process should I expect from your UX agency?

Our UX process is thorough and collaborative, designed to ensure we deeply understand your goals and user needs before moving into design and development. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Discovery: We begin with user research, competitive analysis, and workshops to understand your vision, audience, and objectives.
  • Strategy: We define a product strategy, including user personas, user journeys, and information architecture to guide the design.
  • Design: Our team crafts wireframes, prototypes, and visual designs that align with your brand while prioritizing usability and functionality.
  • Testing: Usability testing is conducted to gather feedback from real users, ensuring the design effectively meets their needs.
  • Iteration & Development: We refine the design based on feedback and work closely with developers to ensure a smooth implementation.

This process allows us to create tailored, user-centered solutions that not only look great but also drive business results.
